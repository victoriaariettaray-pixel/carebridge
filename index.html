CareBridge
Calm intake. Clearer triage.

Modern triage without the cold, rigid feel.

CareBridge is a calmer, more human intake experience that routes patients into a clean provider workflow. It is designed to feel reassuring on the patient side and efficient on the clinical side.

Key talking points
  • I wanted it to feel calmer and more human than a typical rigid portal.
  • The patient side focuses on clarity, while the provider side focuses on speed and prioritization.
  • The goal is not to replace judgment. It is to reduce friction before care begins.
What makes this different
  • Two-role experience instead of one generic screen for everyone.
  • Simple enough to demo live, but structured enough to show product thinking.
